GripTape

GripTape is an educational non-profit organization dedicated to giving youth across the United States the resources they need to pursue learning outside the confines of the classroom. Because of their financial generosity through their Challenger program, Project Mislead was created. Find out more information at www.griptape.org.



Student Voice

Student Voice is a nationwide student-led nonprofit organization that works to equip students to advocate for solutions to educational inequity and social issues within their communities. As a part of their 2019 Ambassadors cohort, the Project Mislead team gained the coalition building, fundraising, and activism skills necessary to manage an initiative of this magnitude. Learn more at www.stuvoice.org.
