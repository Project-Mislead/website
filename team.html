National Director

Manu Onteeru

Manu Onteeru is a freshman at Dartmouth College studying biology, concentrating in environmental health, and computer science. For the past six years, he’s been passionate about the intersection of policy and health services. As an avid debater, namely a former member of the National Speech and Debate Association’s Team USA program and the 2020 National Champion in Congressional Debate, his experience in both public speaking and activism will serve as the foundation for his work with Project Mislead.

Director of Operations

Havish Malladi

Havish Malladi is a freshman at Duke University studying Computer Science and Economics. He has served as a leader of his high school’s computer team for 2 years and was a member of the Math and Science Olympiad teams. As head intern for a start-up for 3 years, Havish has a great deal of experience developing sophisticated business plans and managing a focused group of individuals.

Director of Technology

Khushi Chawla

Khushi Chawla is a first year undergraduate student studying Computer Science and Economics at the University of Virginia. She has been coding for 6 years and has experience with Java, Python, JavaScript (Node.js and React.js), HTML, CSS and GoLang. She volunteered for the non-profit Robotics for Youth for 3 years and worked as the webmaster at the Virginia state-level for the non-profit Kids Are Scientists Too; both organizations aim to teach kids STEM.
