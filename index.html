Ending lead and heavy metal poisoning by

Every family should know about dangers in their homes, schools, water, and playground - and most importantly, how to avoid them. Help us give them the tools they need to build safer futures.

Making lead and heavy metal poisoning yesterday’s problem.

Project Mislead is a first-of-its-kind national initiative to educate others about lead and heavy metal poisoning in communities everywhere. Our philosophy is simple: use existing coalitions and support systems to spread the word about the so-called “silent epidemic” wreaking havoc across homes in America. We position students, activists, and community leaders as educators to make lead poisoning history.
