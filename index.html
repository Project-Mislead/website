Project Mislead is a national initiative to educate others about lead and heavy metal poisoning in communities everywhere. Our philosophy is simple: use existing coalitions and support systems to spread the word about the so-called “silent epidemic” wreaking havoc across homes in America. We position students, activists, and community leaders as educators to make lead poisoning history.

Education

Using materials supported by experts and industry leaders on Project Mislead’s team, our community ambassadors work in their localities to teach others.

Engagement

We create authentic interactions with everyone we meet, from students to teachers to parents to government officials.
