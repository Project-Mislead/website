mission statement

Project Mislead is a nation-wide, not-for-profit initiative to organize and partner with grassroots activists to educate others about lead and heavy metal poisoning in localities and states. We are built on the philosophy of empowering those in local communities by providing educational strategies and materials in order to foster growth - it is unique in this sense because no nationwide organization exists which focuses exclusively on education as a means for heavy metal poisoning prevention.
